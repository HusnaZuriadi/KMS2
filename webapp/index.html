About Al-Kauthar Kindergarten

Al Kauthar Eduqids is a kindergarten established in September 2013 which offers preschool for 4-6 year old students with Pre Tahfiz (Quranic memorization) program, Montessori service program, and also Islamic Advancement program (KAFA). This has made Al Kauthar Eduqids unique and special since not many other kindergarten offer such program to these ‘special needs children’.
